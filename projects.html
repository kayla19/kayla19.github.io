Sumo Battle Robot

This project involved building a robot on a breadboard with an ATMega microcontroller. The peripherals to operate the robot included a battery pack, 4 servo motors, 3 separate sensors, LEDs and more. The project included wiring the robots, assigning each peripheral to a port on the microcontroller, calibrating the wheels so they turned at the same speed, and coding it all on C in the Arduino terminal. Afterwards, the robots were pitted against one another and was written about on the school website here.
